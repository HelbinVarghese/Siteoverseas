Clovers Overseas Studies Pvt. Ltd, explore beyond imagination, is one of the Clovers Group of Companies units having its registered office at Clovers Blue Tower, Thengodu post, Kakkanad, Ernakulam, India. In addition to Clovers Overseas Studies Pvt.Ltd, Clovers Group of Companies comprises of Clovers Safety Services Pvt.Ltd, Clovers Inspira Pvt.Ltd and Clovers IT Park Pvt Ltd. Clovers Overseas Studies Pvt.Ltd is an internationally reputed educational consultancy firm having corporate partners in the UK, USA, Dubai, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Oman, India, Ghana, Nigeria, South Africa, Srilanka, Philippines, and Nepal. Clovers Overseas Studies Pvt.Ltd is managed by a team of professionals headed by Dr. Saju Mathew, an award-winning author, motivational speaker, entrepreneur, safety, and leadership trainer who has trained more than 53000 students and more than 100 corporates across the globe. Clovers Overseas Studies deal with 700 plus universities and employers across 29 countries. Vision To become the best global educational consultancy. Mission To introduce global education opportunities to all classes of people across the globe.
